Starview Valley

Starview Valley is a game that mimicks an RPG (role-playing game) and contains numerous functions and tasks. In this game, you have a mission given by your grandfather, wherein you will take care of the farm he has entrusted to you. Of course, you will also need to defend yourself from invaders that can harm your farm. The game will end once your energy has run out or when you have been defeated by the enemies. Goodluck!!
