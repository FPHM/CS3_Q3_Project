My dear grandchild,

I hope my letter finds you well. I am now in the last of my days, and by the time you read this I'll be gone.

I do not have much by way of material possessions, but I would like to leave you that which I hold dearest - my farm.

I will be honest with you: the situation is dire. You have 30 days - beginning today - to come up with 2,500 gold to pay the bank for the loan I took to cover my hospital bills. Otherwise, the farm will be repossessed.

I am sorry for placing this burden on you, my dear. The days ahead will be tough, but I am certain that greater rewards are in store for you if you're able to soldier through.

I have no money to help you get started, but you can forage in the Cindersap Forest for items that you can sell in the town plaza. After which, head to my friend Pierre's shop and buy seeds for planting.

The cave in the mountains can be mined for valuable gems that you can likewise sell. But be forewarned: these are very few and far between, and there are monsters at every turn. And although more gold can be had in the lower levels of the mine, remember that the deeper you go, the more dangerous it gets. Seek the assistance of the adventurer's guild there before you enter the cave.

The life of a farmer is one of patience and perseverance. Tend to your plants religiously and take time to get to know them. I promise your hard work will pay off.

Starview Valley was my home for most of my life, and I hope you'll come to call it yours, as well. I am at peace knowing that the farm is in your hands.

Love,

Grandpa
